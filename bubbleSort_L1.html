What is Bubble Sort?

Bubble Sort Bubble Sort: A simple sorting algorithm that repeatedly steps through the list, compares adjacent elements and swaps them if they are in the wrong order. is the simplest sorting algorithm. It works by repeatedly Bubbling Bubbling: The process where the largest unsorted element moves to its correct position at the end of the array in each pass. up the largest element to its correct position.

💡 Real-World Analogy: Think of bubbles 🫧 rising to the surface of a liquid. The largest (lightest) bubbles reach the top first.

3. Swap & Repeat

Swap the elements to move the larger one to the right.

int temp = arr[j];
arr[j] = arr[j + 1];
arr[j + 1] = temp;

Step-by-Step Manual Run-Through

How Bubble Sort Works

  • Compare: Check adjacent elements (index j and j+1).
  • Swap: If the left element is larger than the right, swap them.
  • Bubble: Repeat until the largest value "bubbles" to the end of the unsorted part.
